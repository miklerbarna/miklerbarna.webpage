The Vegetarian Diet

Probably the most known diet right now is the vegetarian diet. Some studies suggest that vegetarians take up 18% of the global population. The diet prohibits any type of animal meat from our meals. Basic vegetarianism does not exclude dairy products, eggs or other animal products, it only takes the meat off the plate. The diet claims that we actually do not need meat in our daily lives, but rather we should include more vegetables into our meals.

Benefits of the Diet:

Aside from the ethical and environmental benefits, why is leaving meat off our plate is a good thing? Benefits include:

  • Enchanced weight loss
  • Reduction in the risk of cancer
  • Blood sugar stabilization
  • Improved heart health

Downsides of the Diet:

It is important to still consume enough proteins and vitamins, that are mainly found in meats. By not taking in these nutrients, one can develop deficiencies in calcium, iron or vitamin B12. A nutritional deficiency in these key micronutrients can lead to symptoms like fatigue, weakness, anemia, bone loss or thyroid issues.

Different types of vegetarians:

Diets always have variations and this fact is no different in the case of a vegetarian diet.
